People

The people of Myanmar are very diverse, consisting of over 130 ethnicities. The majority ethnic group are the Bamar people, who mostly reside along the Irrawaddy River and coastal region, including the region of Mandalay. With over 130 ethnicities, there are many languages and dialects spoken across the country, but the official language of the country is Burmese.

Religion

Buddhism is the most widely practiced religion in Myanmar. Over nine-tenths of the population practice Buddhism. The Buddhism that is practiced in Myanmar is called Theravada Buddhism, and almost all the Buddhists in the country practice it. Other religions that make up one-tenth of the population are Christianity, Islam, and Hinduism.
